Maharashtra

Travel Destinations

  • Mumbai - The bustling capital city
  • Pune - Cultural hub and educational center
  • Aurangabad - Gateway to Ajanta and Ellora caves
  • Lonavala and Khandala - Picturesque hill stations
  • Nashik - Famous for its vineyards and temples
  • Elephanta Island - UNESCO World Heritage Site

Itinerary

Here's a suggested itinerary for your trip to Maharashtra:

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Mumbai, explore the city's iconic landmarks such as Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Juhu Beach.
  2. Day 2: Take a day trip to Elephanta Island to visit the Elephanta Caves.
  3. Day 3: Travel to Lonavala and Khandala, enjoy the scenic beauty and visit attractions like Bhushi Dam and Karla Caves.
  4. Day 4: Proceed to Pune, visit historical sites like Shaniwar Wada and Aga Khan Palace.
  5. Day 5: Explore the Ajanta and Ellora caves in Aurangabad, marvel at the ancient rock-cut architecture.
  6. Day 6: Visit Nashik, known for its temples and vineyards, and enjoy wine tasting tours.
  7. Day 7: Departure from Mumbai.
